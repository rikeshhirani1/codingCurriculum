About

The Coding Curriculum Project, now in its Second Year of running, organises all the BSc and MEng Computer Science first years to work in teams to develop and publish programming materials for school children. The publication is in the form of chapters, each on a particular topic that can be combined into a coherent publication such as a book or website.

The emphasis of each chapter is to present the following three progression outlines:
1. Scaffolding - the facts and basic principles of programming
2. Discovery - how you can apply the scaffolding to work on small examples and experiments, to reinforce the learning
3. Innovation - tips and tricks, to show what has been mastered in programming, and can be explained to other learners to help them reach that far

Each Team was assigned to work with one of the following three languages:
1. HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript
2. Microsoft TouchDevelop and our Engduino (C++)
3. Python with or without the Raspberry Pi
